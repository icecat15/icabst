About

       2019 International Conference on Advances in Biological Science and Technology is organized by Asia-Pacific Association of Natural Science and Engineering, will be held in Bangkok, Thailand during December 26-28, 2019.

       As an annual gathering, it provides an extensive platform for scientists, researchers and scholars to present their research results and newest findings in all fields of Biological Science and Technology, discuss the practical challenges encountered recommend better solutions for biotechnology. ICABST2019 will include keynote speeches, invited speeches, oral presentations and poster presentations. The conference certification will be issued to all attendees including listeners.

       Please circulate ICABST2019 to the attention of any of your colleagues who may be interested in participating (click to download conference flyer). We sincerely invite you to join us and hope you will have a fruitful experience during your stay in Thailand!

Topics

Researchers and scholars are encouraged to submit original and unpublished contributions. All submitted papers will go through the peer review process, and acceptance will be based on the quality, originality, language and relevance.

The topics of interest include but are not limited to:

1. Molecular Biology

2. Biomedicine, Biopharmaceuticals and Pharmacology

3. Biomechanics and Biomechanical Engineering

4. Biomaterials and Biomedical materials

5. Biochemistry

6. Agricultural, Food, Animal and Plant Science

7. Other Related Topics

About Presentations

       ICABST2019 will include keynote speeches, invited speeches, oral presentations and poster presentations. The conference certification will be issued to all attendees including listeners. All scientists, scholars, researchers are encouraged to participate the conference for further communication on latest researches, technologies and advanced topics.

       If anyone who want to make presentation, please finish the submission by Online Submission System. The committee will reply in 7-10 working days.

Liability and Insurance

       The conference and organizers are not liable for any injury or damage involving persons and property during the conference. Participants are strongly advised to take out their own personal travel and health insurance for their trip.
