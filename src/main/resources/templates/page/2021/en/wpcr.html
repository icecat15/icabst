International Workshop on Pharmaceutical and Clinical Research WPCR2020
ICABST2021 Workshop

      "International Workshop on Pharmaceutical and Clinical Research WPCR2021" is a continuation of WPCR series, which will be held simultaneously with ICABST 2021 during September 28-30, 2021.
      WPCR2021 focuses on the basic concept, scientific research design, production technology and clinical application in the field of pharmacy, emphasizing the popularization and improvement of pharmaceutical science and technology; while focusing on the comprehensive, basic and practical scientific and technological achievements and advanced experience, and providing a professional platform for researchers and practitioners at home and abroad to promote information exchange and communication in related fields.

① Topics include but not limited to

Industrial pharmacy, Biochemical pharmaceutical, Pharmaceutical preparations, Pharmacological toxicology;
Clinical application, Experimental and Clinical Pharmacology, Adverse reactions, Pharmacokinetics;
Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical biology, Pharmaceutical analysis and control, Pharmaceutical and biomedical analysis;
Drug delivery, Pharmaceutical technology, Pharmaceutical chemistry, Pharmaceutical care;
Pharmaceutical services, Pharmacoeconomics, Pharmaceutical management;
Human anatomy, Embryology, Immunology, Pathology, Epidemiology, Toxicology
Pediatrics, geriatrics, Neurology, Oncology, Physical therapy, Nursing, Anesthesiology;
Imaging Medicine and Nuclear Medicine, Clinical Laboratory Diagnostics

② Workshop Information

Conference Dates: September 28-30, 2021 (The same with ICABST2021)
Abstract Submission Deadline: Oct. 10, 2020
Registration Deadline: Oct. 15, 2020
Acceptance: 7-10 working days
Publications All abstracts/full papers will be sent for peer review by two or more experts in related fields, and the decision will return in about 7-10/15-20 working days. Possible decisions on a manuscript are: acception, revision (minor or major), rejection. We firmly believe that ethical conduct is the most essential virtual of any academic. Hence any act of plagiarism is a totally unacceptable academic misconduct and cannot be tolerated.

***Please be advised that only permitted by authors, could the abstrcts/papers be published.***
Accepted abstracts could be published in the supplement issue of the journal "Basic & Clinical Pharmacology & Toxicology" and collected by Science Citation Index Expanded (Clarivate Analytics).

Selected high quality/excellent full papers could be published in related journals (example journals below) after peer-review.
             
The full papers could also be selected to be published in the proceeding journal and collected by EI Compendex & CPCI-S(ISTP).
